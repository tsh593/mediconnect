## Download Tips

1. **Filter Settings on Sketchfab**:
   - âœ… Check "Downloadable"
   - âœ… License: "Free" or "CC0" or "CC BY"
   - âœ… Format: GLTF or GLB

2. **Download Steps**:
   - Click on a model
   - Click "Download" button
   - Select "GLTF" or "GLB" format
   - Save to `mediconnect/frontend/public/models/`
   - Rename to match the filename above

3. **File Size**:
   - Try to keep models under 10MB each
   - GLB format is usually smaller (single file)
   - GLTF format can be multiple files (model + textures)

4. **Testing**:
   - After downloading, restart your dev server
   - Click on the specialty card
   - The 3D model should load!
